About me

Hi there, it's me Maya

First off, I want to let you know that I am so grateful that you are here. My aim is to tell the story of your love and laughter through images that are timeless. I believe in storytelling through details and emotional portraits. I am all about capturing those raw, honest and beautiful moments of your relationship that shows who you really are. I believe that photos should capture emotion that make you feel something when you look at them.

In addition to these above, I also love hiking in the forests, swimming and diving in the seas, growing my tomatoes, sleeping under the stars, waking up to birds chirping by the window, playing board games, horse riding and going on adventures, big or small at every opportunity. And mostly, I love sharing these experiences with the people I love.
